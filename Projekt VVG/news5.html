RELIVE THE 8 HOURS OF BAHRAIN WITH “THE RED LINE”

The epilogue to a year marked by prestigious results in the FIA WEC 2023 took place at the Sakhir track, the venue of the 8 Hours of Bahrain. The latest episode of “The Red Line”, the series that tells the behind-the-scenes story of the Ferrari – AF Corse team, looks back at an event held on the circuit in the Persian Gulf in early November.

In the last act of the season, the Ferrari 499Ps came third and sixth respectively, with crews 50 and 51, Fuoco-Molina-Nielsen and Pier Guidi-Calado-Giovinazzi.

The team’s arrival in Bahrain, the colours and atmosphere of the country that hosted the last world championship event, the build-up to the race, and the farewell to 2024 are the focus of the new episode, which is available on the Ferrari YouTube channel. “The Red Line” thus recounts the last days of a season where Ferrari took second place in the FIA WEC Manufacturers’ standings, and the crews of the 499P number 50 and 51 finished third and fourth respectively in the Drivers’ standings.
